Garlic Bread

Amazingly good garlic bread made from scratch!

Ingedients
  • 2 Tablespoons butter, softened
  • 1 clove garlic, crushed
  • 2 tablespoons grated Parmesan cheese
  • ground black pepper to taste
  • 1/4 large baguette, sliced
  • 1 teaspoon chopped fresh parsley, to taste
Steps
  1. Preheat grill
  2. Mix together buttle, garlic, parmesan, pepper in a bowl
  3. Spread the butter generously on the baguette slices
  4. Places slices on the preheated grill until butter is melted and slightly brown, about 30 seconds
  5. Sprinkle with parseley
